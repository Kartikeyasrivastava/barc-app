PINAK (Pighalith IndhaN ebam Aub Kriya)

PINAK code is a robust two-dimensional CFD code project to investigate the premixing phase of vapour explosion phenomena in fuel-coolant interaction during a simulated accident condition for Nuclear Reactors with particular emphasis on the configuration of Indian Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWRs). The initial code is jointly developed by Jadavpur Univeristy and Bhabha Atomic Research Centre. PINAK v1.0 is developed jointly by Indian Institute of Technology Madras, SRM Institute of Science and Technology Kattankulathur and Bhabha Atomic Research Centre.
